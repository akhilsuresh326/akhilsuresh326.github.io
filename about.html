akhil suresh is a cinematographer dedicated to exploring the interplay of light, shadow and emotion to craft compelling visual narratives. he holds a diploma in cinematography from new wave film school, kozhikode, where his technical expertise and artistic vision were honed under the mentorship of prathap joseph, a renowned director and cinematographer.

his portfolio showcases a diverse range of fiction and non-fiction projects, including shorts, docs, and features. his unique style blends experimental and naturalistic approaches through nuanced lighting, composition, and a keen eye for detail.

committed to pushing the boundaries of visual storytelling, he continues to explore new ways to create meaningful cinema.
